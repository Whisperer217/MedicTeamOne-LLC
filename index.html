Training Disclaimer & Legal Protection
Civilian Responder One (Basic) is a preparedness training program and not a state or nationally recognized EMS certification. Instruction is based on published guidelines from established medical organizations and is designed for civilian emergency response preparedness.

Participation does not authorize attendees to represent themselves as licensed medical providers or to deliver EMS care. Training focuses on civilian emergency response and preparedness applications.
Good Samaritan Protection
Under Texas law (Texas Civil Practice & Remedies Code §74.151 – Good Samaritan Act), individuals who in good faith provide emergency aid are protected from liability except in cases of willful or wanton negligence. All certificates include legal notation for documentation purposes.
